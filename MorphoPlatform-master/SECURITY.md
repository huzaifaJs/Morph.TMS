## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in this project, please report it by emailing **security@example.com**. 

Please include as much detail as possible about the vulnerability and steps to reproduce it. 
We will acknowledge your report within **2 business days** and provide updates on our investigation at least every **5 business days**.
Once the issue is resolved or if it is declined, we will notify you of the outcome and discuss coordinated disclosure if applicable.
